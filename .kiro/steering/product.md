# Product Overview

**Sanctuary Tour** is an interactive spiritual journey app for church visitors. Users point their camera at sacred artwork within a church to identify it and receive audio narrations about its spiritual meaning and history.

## Core Features

- **Image Recognition**: Uses Gemini AI to identify religious artwork from camera captures
- **Multi-language Audio Tours**: Generates translated narrations with text-to-speech in 8+ languages
- **Interactive Audio Player**: Visual waveform display with play/pause controls
- **Mobile-First Design**: Optimized for on-site church tours via smartphone

## User Flow

1. Home screen with tour instructions
2. Camera capture to photograph artwork
3. AI analysis to identify the piece
4. Result screen with title, description, and language selector
5. Auto-generated audio narration in selected language

## Target Audience

Church visitors seeking deeper understanding of religious art and spiritual heritage, with support for diverse language communities (English, Mandarin, Cantonese, Punjabi, Spanish, French, Italian, German).
